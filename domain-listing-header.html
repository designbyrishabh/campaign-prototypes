Hierarchy fixes applied across all options
  • Vertical separator (1×22px, #e5e7eb) between Mode and action buttons — visually groups the cluster.
  • 16px gap between secondary actions and the primary CTA — gives the hero room to breathe.
  • Secondary buttons demoted to ghost (no border, no fill) so they read as tertiary against the filled CTA.
  • CTA verb changed to Submit for Vetting — more accurate (vetting is the outcome, upload is the mechanism) and eliminates the duplicate "upload".
  • Tooltips on every icon-only button so meaning isn't carried by glyph alone.
  • Sentence case on the CTA label — uppercase felt loud next to ghost-button labels.
