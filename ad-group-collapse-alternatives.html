Recommendation

For a campaign builder, my top pick is Option 1 (clickable header) — it's the cleanest visually, gives a huge click target, and pairs well with the new "Actions" button on the right. If you need a more visible affordance, go with Option 2 (+/−) — it's the most universally understood non-chevron pattern and matches Notion / Linear / Stripe. Skip Option 7 (handle) and Option 6 (rail) for desktop-first dashboards; they're better suited to mobile or sidebars.
